Wan Muhammad Atef Ayang

A boy filled with deep sorrow

Since you've been gone, my life no longer brightens as it used to, everything feels dark and off. I've lost the spirit in my life, lost confidence, lost everything after you left. Your absence has had a profound impact on me. I miss you so much, your voice, your laughter, the charm in your anger. I long for your reactions, even your moments of anger. I miss everything about you. You mean everything to me; I feel a stronger connection with you than with my own family. Every shared experience, I yearn for those moments. Every night, I wait for your phone call; your voice has the power to change everything. When I'm low-spirited or in a bad mood, your voice can bring me back to life. I miss you dearly, Alia. Demi Allah, I love you more than I love myself. You need to know that I am a man who sincerely loves you, willing to sacrifice anything for you. Sumpah lillah Alia, I have no intention of hurting you, forgetting you, hating you, or leaving you. You mean a lot to me. I truly need your heart's space to place this sincere love of mine. I promise not to be a careless man and to become better than before. I love you deeply, and I can't bear to lose you. I need your trust, and I need you. Semoga awak tidak pernah melupakam saya, lelaki yang pernah menjadi sebahagian hidup awak dan saya berharap perkara itu akan berlaku sekali lagi. You are my first love and will be my forever love. Sayangku, i perlukan awak. Sampai sekarang saya tidak boleh melupakan dan sampai bila bila saya tidak boleh melupakan awak. I miss you my sayangku. You are my best home, sayangku.
